Roadmap… Next Turn Adulthood is a fresh comical look at friendship, life choices and working your way through young adulthood while starting an outback tour company.

Episode One

We meet Brie and Mili - Miserable, stuck in mundane, disheartening day jobs. It’s time they did something exciting with their lives. The dream of starting up their own Australian tour company is born.

Episode Two

After being knocked back by the bank for a loan to buy a van, Mili & Brie search for jobs they can do to make fast cash. Brie is forced to face her fear of men when Mili set’s her up on the “perfect” date.

Episode Three

The girls and Jeff set off into the bush to test their skills. They quickly learn they know very little about camping and the outdoors. This weekend test’s their faith and First Aid kit.

Episode Four

With a week until their first tour and no van in sight, the girls panic and decide to raise the missing funds through a carwash. Brie’s “Perfect Date” returns throwing her into a fluster. The carwash is a failure. The girls are out of options until Brie’s father offers a final solution.

Episode Five

Out in the middle of nowhere, the three meet Don an eccentric bushman who offers his van at the cost of a 2 day trek through the wilderness. The trek pushes the girls to their limits both physically and mentally. Will they get their van in time to run their first tour or will this be the ultimate setback?
